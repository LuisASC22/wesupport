Our goal

Here at We-Support we hope to educate users by teaching them about what environmentally
safe practices their favorite companies are (or aren't!) using. We also provide information on what
stances companies are taking in social justice issues.

Why this matters

We must work together to gain a better understanding of eachother and this planet that we call home.
Hopefully, this site will help you see which businesses you should be giving your business to,
to encourage whatever good practices that the company is implementing.
